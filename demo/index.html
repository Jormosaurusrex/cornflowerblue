Form Response

Buttons

Normal

Icons can be placed on either side.

Mute

Mute buttons don't have borders in the resting state.

Hot

Hot buttons are dramatic. The heat() method will turn on hot phase, and cool() will turn that off.

Shaped: Square

Shaped buttons only have icons. They can also be hot or mute.

Shaped: Circle

Shaped buttons only have icons. They can also be hot or mute.

Shaped: Hexagon

Shaped buttons only have icons. Hexagon buttons don't have other states. These aren't recommended for use day-to-day use.
